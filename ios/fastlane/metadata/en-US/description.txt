Free video compression tool that will save you disk space by shrinking your video files sizes.

Features:
- Import video files from different sources
- Select new video dimensions/resolution and compression speed
- Advanced mode to select the bitrate, framerate, volume, and time
- Offline processing for maximum privacy; your files are never uploaded to the internet.
- Works with iPhone, iPad, and iPod
- Works with different video formats: mp4, mov, avi, mkv, wmv, and many more
- Works with different video resolutions: 4K, 1080p, 720p
- Simple and easy to use with a sleek design

The compressed videos are saved to your Files App.
